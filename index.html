The Fairy and the Acorn

In the fairy tale written by Caroline, an elderly lady plants an acorn, the seed of an ancient oak called Skyrack. That night she dreams that a fairy waves her wand over the acorn and sees it as a fully grown oak with all the benefits the tree has brought in its one thousand years life. The Octavia Story Mural shows how the acorn planted in Wisbech in the 1830s led to the oak tree of the National Trust.
